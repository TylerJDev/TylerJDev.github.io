A way to learn more about you and your contacts
Appalyze was created to show you chat data in an easy-to-use, well designed format. Appalyze challenged me to rely only on building a frontend, without a backend.

I wanted to make a service where you wouldn’t have to rely on uploading any data to a server. That’s why I also made it open-source so anyone could inspect, fork, or just keep a copy for themselves locally.
